About the Club

A small parcel of wonder,
delivered to your door each month.

Small Print Press is a monthly pass-it-along print club for people who believe the best things are often the most overlooked. Each month, you'll receive a hand-carved linocut print — something small, detailed, and made with care — inspired by the important stuff that's often overlooked.

Every envelope also contains a postcard version of the print, designed to be sent on to someone you're thinking of. Because the best way to stay connected isn't always a text message — sometimes it's something you can hold.

An original print
A hand-carved linocut on quality paper, signed and ready to frame or pin up.
A pass-along postcard
A postcard of the print to send to someone you've been meaning to reach out to.
A letter
A few words about the print, the inspiration behind it, and the small wonder it celebrates.
